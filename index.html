DESCRIPTION:
Place and step on arrows so they can throw you in the direction they are pointing at. Get coins to earn points!

TIPS:
* Stepping on an arrow increases the multiplier.
* Picking up a coin cashes in the multiplier.

BEWARE:
* Stepping on tile of the other character resets the multiplier!
